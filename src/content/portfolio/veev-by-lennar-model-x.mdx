We partnered with **Veev**, one of the **Bay Area**'s leading design-build builders, supporting residential projects across the region.

With more than **100 homes** completed each year, **Veev** requires a cabinet manufacturing partner that can keep pace with demanding construction schedules while maintaining consistent quality. As a local California manufacturer, we worked closely with the builder throughout the project, from design coordination and production to delivery and installation support.

Our team responded quickly to design changes and job site needs, helping keep every stage of the project moving smoothly.

For large-scale builder projects, local manufacturing makes a real difference. Faster communication, reliable lead times, and responsive service help ensure projects stay on schedule without compromising quality.
